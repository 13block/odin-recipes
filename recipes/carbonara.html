Carbonara Pasta

Description

Carbonara pasta is a dish originated from Italy

It is a mix of pasta with a sauce composed of guanciale

Ingredients

  • Linguini pasta
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Guanciale
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs and the pepper in a bowl
  2. Add the pecorino and mix it
  3. In the meantime, cook the pasta and the guanciale
  4. When everything is ready, mix it all together
