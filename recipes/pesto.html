Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a dish originated from Italy

It is a mix of pasta with a sauce composed of cheese and basil

Ingredients

  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Parmesan or Romano cheese
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Pine nuts
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pulse the basil and pine nuts
  2. Add the garlic and cheese
  3. Slowly pour in the olive oil
  4. Season the pesto sauce
