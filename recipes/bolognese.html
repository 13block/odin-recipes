Bolognese Pasta

Description

Bolognese pasta is a dish originated from Italy

It is a mix of pasta with a sauce composed of tomato sauce and meat

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Wine
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sodium beef
  • Leaf
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Cook the beef
  3. Simmer the meat and milk
  4. Add the wine
  5. Crush and add the tomatoes
  6. Serve
